Packages

Three ways to engage.

What's included

One full Market Signal Report covering a single zip code or one defined geography of your choosing (a neighborhood, submarket, or custom radius). Includes tier classification, the top signals driving the score, full investor ratios, and a comparison against the broader metro.

Who it's for

An investor or lender with one specific opportunity already on the table — you have a zip code or two in mind and want a fast, data-backed second opinion before committing capital or underwriting time.

Vs. Comparative

Comparative gets you the same depth across 3 geographies with a side-by-side ranking — this tier is for validating one thesis, not choosing between several.
