How we build the signal.

SpotWise's momentum score is generated by a machine learning model trained on a range of economic and real estate indicators measured at the zip code level. The model is walk-forward validated — trained only on data available before each prediction point, then tested against real outcomes over five-year holding periods. Top-tier picks beat their own metro 94% of the time over five years.

The dashboard also includes a buyer-adjustable weighting tool that lets investors re-rank zip codes by their own priorities — cash flow, appreciation, or a balanced view. This is a filtering layer on top of the base score, not a separate model: the underlying momentum score is fixed and validated; the re-ranking simply reorders results to match how a specific buyer evaluates deals.

Coverage today: residential and small multifamily properties (1–4 units) across the New York, New Jersey, and Pennsylvania tri-state area — not large multifamily, commercial, or industrial assets.
